Recruitment Policy

By submitting an application, you agree to the following policies:

1. Applicants must be mature, responsible, and respectful toward the community.

2. Any abuse of staff permissions will result in permanent removal.

3. Staff members must maintain confidentiality of system data and internal tools.

5. Staff are required to remain active and assist users daily.

6. Administrators reserve the right to reject any application without explanation.

7. Providing false information will permanently disqualify the applicant.

8. Policies may change at any time without prior notice.

9. Final decision belongs to the system administrator.

10. By clicking submit, you confirm you fully accept all rules and consequences.
